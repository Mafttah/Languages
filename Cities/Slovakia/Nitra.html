Welcome to Nitra

Nitra is Slovakia's oldest city with settlements dating to the Stone Age and documented history from the 9th century as seat of the first Slovak bishopric. The imposing castle complex on Zobor Hill, historic churches, and agricultural university character define the city. Nitra represents Slovak Christianity's beginnings and Great Moravian heritage. The combination of ancient roots, religious significance, and modern university life creates a city where Slovakia's origins meet contemporary vitality.
