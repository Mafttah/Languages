Welcome to Daugavpils

Daugavpils is Latvia's second-largest city in the southeast, featuring a massive 19th-century fortress and multicultural character. The city's Russian-speaking majority, proximity to Belarus and Lithuania, and industrial heritage create distinct identity. The Daugavpils Fortress, one of Europe's best-preserved 19th-century fortifications, and the Mark Rothko Art Centre celebrating the abstract expressionist born here define the city. Daugavpils offers authentic eastern Latvia experience with cultural diversity and historical depth.
