3 Lesson 3 — Regular Verbs Verbs

Regular verbs are divided into 6 categories

1. -Āt Verbs

  • runāt = to speak
  • strādāt = to work
  • domāt = to think
  • dziedāt = to sing
  • mēģināt = to try

    • 2. -Īt Verbs

  • mācīt = to teach
  • uzrakstīt = to write
  • nosūtīt = to send
